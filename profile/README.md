# Worthivo

**Submission for RevenueCat Shipyard: Creator Contest**

Worthivo is a mobile lifestyle assistant built with Flutter and powered by Google Gemini 2.0 Flash, designed to help households manage rising living costs through smarter shopping, cooking, and financial decisions.

---

## The Problem

Persistent inflation has made everyday household management increasingly difficult. Grocery prices fluctuate constantly, food waste accumulates from poor planning, and most consumers lack accessible tools to evaluate whether a purchase, a recipe, or a renovation decision is actually a good use of their money.

## The Solution

Worthivo combines computer vision, generative AI, and structured financial tooling into a single household assistant. Rather than offering generic budgeting tips, the app meets users at the point of decision: while standing in a store aisle, while planning a meal from what's already in the kitchen, or while considering how to spend a renovation budget.

The product is organized around four core pillars:

**Smart Shelf Scanner** — Uses AI vision to identify a scanned product and assess its value relative to price and nutritional quality, giving an immediate buy/skip recommendation.

**Smart Kitchen** — Generates recipes from ingredients the user already has on hand, reducing food waste and turning pantry leftovers into usable meals, with AI-assisted customization.

**Financial Tools** — Scans receipts to track spending automatically, models simple investment scenarios through a savings simulator, and analyzes home renovation plans against a defined budget.

**Monetization** — A freemium structure built on RevenueCat, with monthly and annual subscription tiers that unlock expanded AI usage limits.
